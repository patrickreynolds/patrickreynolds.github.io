Since I have no idea what we're supposed to write about in these paragraphs, I'll tell you a little bit about one of my passions - Traveling. I've always wanted to travel abroad, and the summer after my freshman year of college I finally got the opportunity to take the plunge. It was great because I got the opportunity to backback numerous countries and study in Spain for the last half of my trip. I was even lucky enough to see Spain win the world cup while I was there. Yes, the World Cup.

It was pretty awesome to say the least. I constantly get asked what were my favorite countries, and even though it's practically impossible to answer that, to make things less complicated I usually say:

  1. Spain
  2. Germany
  3. Switzerland

All of the countries I visited were awesome in their own way, but those three, in that order, were some of my favorites. Whilst traveling as a poor college student, I was trying to be as thrifty as possible. Outlined below in no particular order are a few methods I suggest to save some money. They all worked for me in their own way.

  • Eurail
  • Hostels
  • Super Markets
  • Couch Surfing
  • Airbnb.com
  • Make lots of friends.

The list above is just a few suggestion I would have for you when looking to travel. If you are thinking about traveling anytime soon, or just want some more info, feel free to email me with any questions!
